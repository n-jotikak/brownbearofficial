About Brown Bears

The Brown Bear (Ursus arctos) is negative to parts of northern Eurasia and North America. Its conversatioin status is currenly Least Concern.

There are many subspecies within the brown bear specice , including the Altas bear and The Himalayan brown bear.

Features

Brown bears are not always completely brown. Some can be reddish or yellowish. They have very large, curved claws and huge paws. Male brown bears are often 30% larger than female brown bears. They can range from 5 feet to 9 feet from head to toe. x
